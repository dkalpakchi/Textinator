Thank you for joining Textinator! Your annotation or evaluation efforts will most certainly help in advancing Natural Language Processing (NLP) beyond its current limits.

If you are here to help annotating, here are some tips:

  • If you are unfamiliar with Textinator or with NLP annotations in general, we have prepared a couple of short YouTube videos to help you in starting this journey .
  • If you have signed up to take part in a specific annotation effort, please check out "My Tasks" tab in the menu above. If you don't see your project there, please contact your project manager.
  • If you have signed up out of curiosity and willigness to help, huge thank you! We are very grateful and honored to have you on board! Please check out "Open Tasks" tab and see if we currently have any annotation efforts, open to general public. We urge you to read the instructions for the annotation task carefully to check that you understand them and are indeed eligible to take part. Please contact the project responsible if any questions arise.

If you are here to help evaluating, here are some other tips:

  • If you have signed up to take part in a specific evaluation effort, please check out "Survey" tab and see if your survey is ready. If you don't see your survey, please contact your project manager.
  • If you have signed up out of curiosity, please check out the very "Survey" tab and see if there are any surveys open to general public. We urge you to read the instructions carefully to check that you are indeed eligible to take part. Please contact the responsible for the survey if any questions arise.
